About Us

We are a group of 4 Final year mechanical engineering students from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala from the class of 2022 who have created this kettle for our final year capstone project under the guidance of our Dr. Jay Prakash Tripathi and Dr. Pankaj Kumar.
We worked on this project for a duration of 2 semesters (6th and 7th) and are really thankful to our college and our professors for keeping such an opportunity as a part of our curriculum where we not only got to apply the knowledge we have learned during the duration of our Bachelor of Engineering but also learned new skills to see this project to it's complition.
