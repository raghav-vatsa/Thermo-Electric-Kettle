What we Do?

We have developed a unnique and innovative kettle which has the ability to not only increase the temerature of the liquid present in it's container but also decrease it's temperature based on the users requirement at the simple push of a button while keeping the design small and simple

The result is a compact, low cost and versatile compliance which saves you money and space for multiple appliances .

Technology

We have developed a unique kettle using Thermoelectric module or Peltier Module which is a thermal control module that has both warming and cooling effects. By passing an electric current through the module, it is possible to change the surface temperature of the module and keep it at the target temperature. So our innovative product can not only heat liquids but also cool them based on the users requirement.
